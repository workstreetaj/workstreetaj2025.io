1

Initiate in HubSpot

Start contract in HubSpot first (NOT Juro)

Enter deal with $0 amount - 'Vanta pass through revenue only'

2

Create Contract in Juro

Use proper naming conventions and complete all required fields

Required Fields (reference the Juro Agreement Summary document):

Company Name (Client field in Agreement Summary)
Trial (Yes/No)
Vanta Price (monthly amount - see calculation note below)
Payment Schedule (Billing Frequency field)
Frameworks (under Vanta Subscription section)
Payment Term (under Duration and Payment Terms)
Service Term (Initial Term in Duration and Payment Terms)
Program (Services field - e.g., VIP free)
Contract Start Date (Commencement date)
Signee Name + Email (under Signed on behalf of [Client])
Company Size (under Vanta Subscription section)
View HubSpot Deal Example
3

Add Vanta Rep as Recipient

Add Vanta Rep (in this case Lucas Dantan) as Recipient in Juro

They'll be notified when customer signs

4

Send for Signing

Wait for client to sign

Ty's signature is pre-loaded - no counter-sign needed

Common Error

Enter MONTHLY cost in Juro, not annual total. Example: Vanta Price $5,000 for 12 months = $5,000 ÷ 12 = $416.67 monthly (enter $416.67 in Juro, NOT $5,000)

Revenue Note

Workstreet doesn't receive direct revenue - recorded as $0. Only Vanta Subscription fees are charged to client.

Provisioning

Vanta handles provisioning (unless MSP arrangement)
